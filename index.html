Why this exists

The scams are Indian. The defenses are not.

Digital-arrest scams alone took an estimated Rs 1,935 crore from Indians in 2024, and victims are disproportionately elderly. Most scam checkers are English-first, cloud-dependent, and blind to the patterns that actually hit Indian phones: fake police video calls, UPI tricks, courier customs threats, deepfake investment reels. JaanchLo is built for exactly that gap.

How it works

Three steps to a plain answer

Share what you got

Paste the message, upload a screenshot (read on your device, never uploaded), or answer the live-call checklist: "am I being scammed right now?"

The SREM engine scores it

48 signals across Authority, Fear, Payment, Channel, and Content. 12 hard knockout rules catch the unmistakable patterns, and false-alarm guards protect real messages from real banks.

Get a verdict and a next step

Safe, Be Careful, or Danger, with the reasons in plain English or Hindi and exactly what to do next, including when to call 1930.

Measured, not claimed

Tested against 3,172 labeled messages

Every number below is reproduced by one command in the open repository. The golden holdout shares no templates with the rest of the data, and expected severities are authored independently of the engine, so the evaluation is not circular.

False alarms on genuine messages0.0%0.0%

The false-alarm rate is the number we guard hardest. A shield that cries wolf gets uninstalled, and then it protects no one.

Honest limits: JaanchLo is a second opinion, not a guarantee. It cannot see who is really calling, and new scam scripts appear every month. When in doubt: do not pay, hang up, and call 1930.
Privacy

Nothing you check ever leaves your phone

No account

No sign-up, no phone number, no OTP. Open it and use it.

On-device analysis

The engine runs in your browser. Screenshots are read locally. There is no server storing your messages.

Verified by tests

The reference API neither logs request bodies nor writes them to disk. That claim is enforced by an automated test in the repository.

Open source · open data

Every claim is inspectable

The detector, the scoring rubric, the 3,172-row EN/HI/Hinglish scam corpus, the tests, and the CI gate are all public. Nothing like this dataset existed openly before. Fork it, audit it, improve it.

About

JaanchLo (जांच लो, "check it first") is a free public-good project. It gives a fast, explainable second opinion. It is not a guarantee and does not replace the police or your bank.

Built in Bengaluru by Regnor. Free forever. No ads, no tracking, no account.
